We are a premier Martial Arts School located in North Kingstown, RI. Our staff of professional Instructors teach students in both Traditional and Competitive Taekwondo. Grand Master Hong's philosophy is that Taekwondo is much more than self-defense. He teaches that its foundation is about mental discipline, confidence, balance, and harmony. Gaining strength and energy in these qualities will not only make one a good student, but also a better person in life.
